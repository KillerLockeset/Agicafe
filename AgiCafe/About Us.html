Agi cafe history

Pedro Fonseca, in his adolescence, lost his father to a respiratory illness. He with his mother and sister needed to pay the bills. His father owned a small bakery and they helped run the business, but without him things would be tight. One day he told his situation to his best friend, Jorge Alcantâra who had just read a book and had been thinking about it a lot, so he came up with the idea of revamping the bakery. And that's when Agi Café was born, a themed cafeteria that enriches your mind and improves your coexistence in society

In honor of the book that gave Home to Agi Café formalizing a quiz about it, test your knowledge in the 1984 book quiz!
